Google Business Profile Optimization Mastery


Complete Profile Setup


Business Information Accuracy:

  • Exact business name matching website
  • Complete address with suite numbers
  • Primary phone number (local area code preferred)
  • Website URL (tracking-enabled for analytics)
  • Accurate business hours including holidays

Category Optimization:

  • Primary: "Personal Injury Attorney"
  • Secondary: "Legal Services", "Lawyer", "Law Firm"
  • Avoid over-categorization (maximum 5 categories)

Review Management Strategy


Review Acquisition Systems


Post-Case Review Requests:

  • Timing: 1-2 weeks after case resolution
  • Platform priorities: Google, Avvo, Facebook
  • Follow-up sequence for non-responders
  • Incentives (where ethically permitted)

Ongoing Review Generation:

  • Email signature review links
  • Website review request placement
  • Social media review campaigns
  • Client newsletter review requests

Review Response Best Practices


Positive Review Responses:

  • Thank the client personally
  • Mention specific case details (if permitted)
  • Include location references
  • Add subtle call-to-action for others

Negative Review Management:

  • Respond professionally and promptly
  • Address concerns without admitting fault
  • Offer to discuss privately
  • Follow up with resolution updates

Review Response Template Examples:


Positive Review Response:

"Thank you, [Client Name], for taking the time to share your experience. We're grateful we could help you navigate your [case type] case here in [City]. Our team is committed to providing exceptional legal representation to all our [City] neighbors. If anyone needs personal injury assistance, we're here to help."


Negative Review Response:

"Thank you for your feedback. We take all client concerns seriously and would appreciate the opportunity to discuss your experience directly. Please contact our office manager at [phone] so we can address your concerns properly. We're committed to providing excellent service to all our [City] clients."
