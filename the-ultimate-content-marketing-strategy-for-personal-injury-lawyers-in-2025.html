Referral Sources (Professional network):

Content Strategy Framework for Personal Injury Law


The TRUST Content Model


Teach - Educational content that informs and guides

Reassure - Content that addresses fears and concerns

Understand - Empathetic content showing you comprehend their situation

Support - Helpful resources and tools

Transform - Content showing positive outcomes and life changes


Content Funnel Strategy


Top of Funnel (Awareness Stage):

  • Educational blog posts about legal processes
  • "What to do after..." guide content
  • Legal rights and process explanations
  • Industry news and legal updates

Middle of Funnel (Consideration Stage):

  • Case study content and success stories
  • Attorney comparison guides
  • Process walkthroughs and timelines
  • FAQ content addressing common concerns

Bottom of Funnel (Decision Stage):

  • Consultation preparation guides
  • Team and attorney profiles
  • Client testimonials and reviews
  • Contact and consultation scheduling content
